A band I'm excited about: Street Sweeper

Tom Morello, the lead guitarist from Rage Against the Machine and Audioslave, has formed a new band; a collaboration with rapper Boots Riley (of The Coup). They’re called “Street Sweeper” and they’re coming out with a new album soon. Also, they’re about to go on tour with Nine Inch Nails and Jane’s Addiction (major win). Tour dates and sample music are online.
